What is MyGALA?

MyGALA is a free website that helps learners explore what to do after school — including scholarships, jobs, and career guidance.

Is MyGALA only for South Africans?

No — while it includes many South African resources, the advice and tools can help any learner planning their next steps after graduation.

Can I contribute to MyGALA?

Yes! If you have a resource, opportunity, or idea to add, use the Contact page to send a message.

Is there a mobile version?

Yes, MyGALA is mobile-friendly and works on any device with a web browser.
