Scholarship Opportunities

  • NSFAS (South Africa): Financial aid for university and TVET college students.
  • Mandela Rhodes Foundation: Postgraduate funding for leadership-focused students.
  • DAAD (Germany): Scholarships for international students studying in Germany.
  • Commonwealth Scholarships: For students from developing countries to study in the UK.
  • Fulbright Program (USA): For postgraduate study in the United States.
